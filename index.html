House Rules

A full guide with rules and instructions will be waiting at the cabin when you arrive. Here's what to know ahead of time.

  • When you arrive: You'll need to turn on the water, hot water heater, and thermostat. Short instructional videos will be sent to you beforehand — it's straightforward.
  • Maximum 6 adults, plus up to 2 children on the bunk bed
  • Pets are allowed, but the property has no fence. Dogs and other pets must be supervised outdoors at all times. Please mention this when you book so we can go over specifics.
  • Quiet after 10 PM
  • No smoking inside
  • Store food properly — we're in bear country
  • Leave it exactly how you found it
  • Tell us right away if anything breaks — no big deal, we just want to know
  • Check out by 1:00 PM and lock up on your way out
  • Take the trash to the outside bins
  • Linens in a pile in the laundry room
Things to do nearby 🏕

You're in California's smallest county — and there's a lot more to do than you might expect. Bear Valley Lodge is just down the road, and the surrounding area has world-class hiking, fishing, skiing, and some of the most beautiful mountain scenery in the state.
